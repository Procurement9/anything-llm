# Evidence Gathering and Cataloging Checklist

## Certified Mail Evidence

When searching for certified mail records and green card return receipts, look for:

**In Gmail (ministryestateandtrust@gmail.com):**
- Search queries: `USPS OR "certified mail" OR "return receipt" OR "green card" OR "tracking"`
- Search queries: `"9400" OR "7010" OR "7012" OR "7014"` (USPS certified mail tracking number prefixes)
- Look for USPS email confirmations with subject lines like "Your item was delivered"
- Look for scanned images of green cards (PS Form 3811) — small green postcard with signature

**In Google Drive:**
- Run: `gws drive files list --params '{"q": "name contains \"certified\" or name contains \"USPS\" or name contains \"receipt\""}'`
- Look for scanned PDFs or JPEGs of postal receipts

## Evidence to Request via Discovery (Bamberg County SC)

File a formal Discovery Request demanding:

| Item | Relevance |
|------|-----------|
| K9 training records (all certifications, false positive rates) | *Florida v. Harris* challenge |
| K9 handler's certification and training logs | K9 reliability |
| Dashcam / bodycam footage of the stop | Stop duration (*Rodriguez*) |
| Dispatch records showing time of stop and duration | Stop duration |
| Officer's notes and incident report | Basis for stop |
| Lab analysis of any substance seized | Challenge identification |
| Chain of custody records | Evidence integrity |
| Officer's prior disciplinary records | Credibility |
